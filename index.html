Advanced Diploma in IT, Networking, and Cloud Computing

The Advanced Diploma in IT, Networking and Cloud Computing being offered as an initiative buoyed by IBM, supported by the Directorate General of Training, and implemented by Edunet Foundation is a 2-year full time NSQF level 6 certified knowledge enhancement diploma aims to tap the vocational workforce into the IT sector through various industries. This initiative includes training in IT, cloud computing and networking, which can be augmented with any respective trade. The initiative is supported by the Directorate General of Training.
